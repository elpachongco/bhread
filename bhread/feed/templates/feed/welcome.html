Features

  • Threaded post and comments using your blog

    Anyone can reply to your post by also posting anything with the phrase "replying to" followed by the URL to your post.

  • Reposts

    Want to share an interesting post? Just paste a link to it! If Bhread recognizes the URL, it will be a repost.

  • Likes

    Registered users can like posts

  • (WIP) Groups

    Create communities using your blog.

  • Per-post comment section for your blog

    Bhread allows use of your posts' own threads as comment section for your blog posts

  • No account needed
