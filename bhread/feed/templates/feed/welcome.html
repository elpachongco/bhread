Welcome to Bhread.com

Bhread is a blog-powered social media. (blog threads)

Basically, we take blog posts and allow other blogs to reply to it.

How?

Simply publish a post containing "replying to " followed by a link. Like this:

replying to bhread.com/welcome

Hello World!

This tells bhread to create a threaded post containing your post as a reply.

Here's an example thread:

Replies are great but it's only one example of Bhread's capabilities. Bhread allows any blogger to:

  • Automatically post new content (using feeds),
  • reply to other bloggers,
  • repost their content,
  • and create groups.

Most importantly, you don't need an account to participate!

You can create an account to customize things but a blog is all you need to participate.
