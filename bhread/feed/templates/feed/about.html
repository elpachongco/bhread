Welcome to Bhread.com

Bhread.com is a blog aggregator that makes threads out of blogs. 'blog threads'

Get started

You need

  • A blog with an RSS or Atom Feed
Register an account to bhread.com and add a feed. Copy the URL of your blog's feed to the form and click submit.

Make a blog post, start it with 'replying to' and a link to some web page e.g. bhread.com:

replying to bhread.com Hello World!

Bhread will politely scan your feed every x seconds and if it finds a post starting with 'replying to <link>', it will make a thread from it. That's it!

Idea

The idea of bhread is to create a centralized hub of interactions without centralizing data.

When I search for something today, I usually use site:reddit.com to find something valuable. For example I google: best anime 2023 site:reddit.com .

I always use reddit.com, sometimes specifying a subreddit because comments there are mostly from people. I'd use facebook if they allowed search engines to crawl their sites but they don't. It's unfortunate because some of the most active and knowledgeable groups I found on the internet is on Facebook.

This is inefficient and it's not how the internet should be. Humanity's knowledge should not belong to a single entity.

This is why bhread was created. I want the same interactions as facebook, reddit, twitter but without the centralization of data.

In theory, bhread solves a lot of social media problems we have today[1]. If you have time, help me test it out. Register your blog feed today on bhread.com. If you don't have a blog, create one for yourself and have your own piece of the internet.

[1] - Although it also introduces new problems (moderation, for example), it's forgivable.
